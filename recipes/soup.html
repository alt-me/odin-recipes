Finnish Summer Soup

Description

his Finnish summer soup should be made with fresh vegetables. Serve it with a bread and cheese platter as they do in Finland. If you can't get fresh shelled green peas, use frozen tiny green peas in this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 6 cups chopped (cut into 3-inch lengths) green onions
  • 5 small potatoes, peeled and havled
  1. Bring water to a boil in a large pot. Add potatoes. Reduce heat; simmer until potatoes are tender, 15 to 20 minutes.
  2. Add onions, green beans, carrots, butter, salt, and black pepper to the pot; simmer until tender, then add peas.
  3. Combine half-and-half and flour in a medium bowl until smooth; stir into simmering vegetables. Cook, stirring constantly, until soup is slightly thickened.
